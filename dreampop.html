Dreampop

Mixed Reality casual game inspired by popping bubble wraps, bubble shooter games and push pop fidget toys!

In Mixed Reality, players can immerse themselves in a world where they can virtually pop bubbles, aiming for high scores and relaxation. The game combines the joy of physical interaction with the convenience of digital entertainment, providing a unique and enjoyable experience for players of all ages.
